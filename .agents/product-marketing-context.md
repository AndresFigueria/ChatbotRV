# Product Marketing Context — Robotina Central

## What We Sell
**Robotina Central** is a WhatsApp AI automation system for restaurants in Latin America.
It handles orders, reservations, customer profiles (CRM), and internal reporting — all through WhatsApp, without the customer needing to download any app.

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** Robotina Central
- **Tagline:** *"Tu restaurante trabaja. Tú descansas."*
- **Tone:** Confident but warm — accessible tech, not intimidating. Speaks like a trusted advisor.
- **Colors:** WhatsApp Green (#25D366) + Electric Orange (#FF6B35) + Deep Black (#0D0D0D)
- **Typography:** Modern, clean — Inter / Outfit
- **Language:** Spanish (Latin America)

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Restaurant owners in Ecuador, Colombia, Perú, México (LATAM)
- **Secondary:** Food businesses: cafeterias, food trucks, catering, dark kitchens

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Restaurant owner, 30–55 years old
- Manages 3–25 employees
- Overwhelmed by: missed orders via WhatsApp, inconsistent staff, lack of real-time data
- Currently using: WhatsApp manually + paper orders + Excel
- Pain: losing money on chaos, not on quality of food
- Dreams of: "que el negocio funcione solo mientras yo descanso"

## Core Jobs-to-be-Done
1. "Take and confirm orders without having a person glued to the phone"
2. "Know in real time what's selling and what's not"
3. "Remember VIP customers without effort"
4. "Close the day knowing exactly what happened"

## Key Pain Points (Voice of Customer)
- "Se me van los pedidos de WhatsApp"

- "No sé cuánto vendí hoy hasta que cuento la caja"
- "A las 11pm yo sigo respondiendo mensajes"
